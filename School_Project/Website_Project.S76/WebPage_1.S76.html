In 2008, MARVEL released it's first movies IRON MAN to start a cinematic univers. In this movie,Tony Stark, who's the owner of Stark Industries was kidnapped by a terrorist gang known as Ten Rings. To escape from there, Tony makes a suit with the help of his new friend Yinsen, capable of fly him out of that place. After coming back from there,Tony upgrades his designs and his company partner Stane, copies his designes and builds a suit for himself.Stane use that suit to kill Tony and own his company. Tony fights Stane in public places and wins company back.After that S.H.E.I.L.D tells Tony to hide his IRON MAN identity from the world in Press Conference but Stark reveales his identity and says- "I AM IRON MAN".
